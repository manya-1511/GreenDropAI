Gopal Rao - Rice Innovator

Bangalore Rural Rice

Key Achievements

  • Increased yield from 4 to 6.5 tons per acre
  • Reduced water usage by 30% with SRI methods
  • Trained 50+ farmers in sustainable practices
  • Established organic rice brand "Hoskote Harvests"

Gopal Rao, a 52-year-old farmer from Hoskote taluk, transformed his 8-acre rice farm through innovative System of Rice Intensification (SRI) techniques. Frustrated with declining yields and increasing input costs, Gopal attended a Krishi Vigyan Kendra workshop in 2018 that changed his approach to farming.

His implementation of alternate wetting and drying (AWD) irrigation reduced water consumption by 30% while maintaining soil health. By switching to organic fertilizers and adopting precise planting techniques (25x25cm spacing), Gopal achieved a remarkable yield increase from 4 to 6.5 tons per acre.

Recognizing his success, the state agriculture department featured Gopal in their "Model Farmers" program. He now hosts monthly field demonstrations and has helped establish a farmers' collective that markets organic rice directly to Bengaluru consumers, fetching 25% higher prices than conventional rice.

Farming Techniques

  • System of Rice Intensification (SRI)
  • Alternate wetting and drying irrigation
  • Organic nutrient management
  • Precision planting (25x25cm spacing)
